And here is my story

 
 

A once bored employee who used to work in a real estate firm, finally decided to quit his job and pursue his dream of becoming a web developer.

Studying almost 9 hours a day, 7 days a week for months, made me able to code beautifully designed websites and web apps that stand out while focusing on creating the best possible user experience.

My passion for front-end web development is because combining development with design satisfies my need for creativity. Making a living through my passion was the best decision I made.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm propably cycling, listening to a new type of music or miserably trying to learn playing guitar.
